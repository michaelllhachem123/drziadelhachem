Who he is

Expertise you can trust, attention you can feel.

Dr. Ziad El Hachem is a specialist in pediatrics and infectious diseases, and a well-known, trusted name for families across the Jbeil region. Parents come to him not only for his expertise, but for the rare quality of his attention.

He listens. He explains. He stays on the line until you can breathe again. His practice rests on a simple conviction: a child's health is too important to rush — so every consultation is given the time it deserves, and every family is treated as if it were his own.

  • Pediatrics from newborn to adolescent
  • Infectious diseases — flu, respiratory & childhood infections
  • Personally reachable for true emergencies, 24/7
